About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Engineering Systems and Design (ESD) Pillar, Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), advised by Prof. Lingjie Duan, since Aug 2024. From Dec 2023 to Jun 2024, I was a Visiting Scholar at The Ohio State University, hosted by Prof. Ness B. Shroff, Prof. Yingbin Liang, and Prof. Sen Lin. I received the Ph.D. degree from SUTD in Jul 2024 and the B.Sc. Degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in Jun 2019, respectively.
