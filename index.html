About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at NSF AI-EDGE Institute, The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Ness B. Shroff and Prof. Yingbin Liang. I earned my Ph.D. from Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) in Jul 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Lingjie Duan and received my B.Sc. Degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in Jun 2019.
